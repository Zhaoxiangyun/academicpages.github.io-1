About Me

Hello! My name is Xiangyun Zhao. I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate studying Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the ECE department @ Northwestern University. My advisor is Professor Ying Wu. I obtained my bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China(UESTC).
I am now in the job market, please feel free to reach out if you are interested.
My research interests include machine learning and computer vision.
I was very fortunate to have interned at 360 AI Institute(Beijing, 2015), Microsoft Research Asia (Beijing, 2016), Adobe Research (San Jose, 2017), Baidu Research (Sunnyvale, 2018) and NEC-Lab(San Jose, 2019). I am currently a reserach intern at Google Research.
